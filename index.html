Shoe Repairs


ALTERATIONS

We offer FREE consultation on all shoe alterations. We resole, re-heel or repair zips on all types of shoes and boots for men and women! We also offer specialist jobs on boot and shoe repairs including shortening or widening of your boots!



SHOE ACCESSORIES

We stock a wide range of shoe accessories including brushes, polishes and creams! We also have a wide selection of soles, heels and leather swatches to suit any repair job for both men and women! In addition we stock a wide range of shoe care products.

Bags Sold & Repairs

We repair wheels, handles, side catches, towing handles, zip locks, combination locks, snaps and hinges on all makes and models. We can also repair cracks in plastic cases and stitch nylon plastic bags! We have a range of suitcases, cabin bags, laptop bags, sling bags, purses, luggage bags and padlocks to purchase in store!

Tailoring & Clothing Alterations

We specialise in alterations of all types of clothes including shirts, trousers, dresses and blouses. We also specialise in indian clothing tailoring from blouses, Gaghras, Cholis, Salwar Kameez, Kurtas and Sherwanis to dresses for boys and girls!


Key Cutting Services & Accessories





KEY RANGE

We sell a wide range of key rings, tags, remotes and have a large selection of keys to suit all needs and uses.



KEY SERVICES

We are capable of handlng all your key duplication needs, both private and corporate. We also cut car keys and programme them at our site.

Other Services




DRY CLEANING

We are collection agents for Dry Cleaners. Clothes returned the same day if dropped before 9 am.



WATCH BATTERIES

We sell and replace your watch battery.




OTHER PRODUCTS WE SELL

We also sell electrical power adapters, wallets and belts.



OTHER REPAIRS

We repair leather goods, jacket lining and zip replacements.
